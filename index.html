Get to Know Me!

Hi there!

My name is Maddie, and I am aspiring to earn a Ph.D in Chemistry to become a professor one day. I have developed many skills as an undergaduate in varying fields. Through courses I have taken and pathways I have paved for myself, I gained experience in

  • Laboratory Research
  • Teamwork
  • Web Development
  • Film Editing
  • Teaching

Among all other things I have learned in my life, I have found that it is very important to be aware of the message you are communicating. As someone interested in a STEM field, I have met a lot of different people from all over the world. I have always been accostumed to broken English, as my parents immigrated to the United States from Italy.

But, it is very clear to me that a lot of people that earn degrees and go on and teach, either don't understand the language very well to properly convey concepts or don't care about their students at all. As a student who wants to teach, I am striving to break that stereotype by using my experience in the classroom as a student to improve as a teacher. It is important to me to effectively get points across when explaining, so I decided to pick up a second major as an undergraduate in Communication & Media.
